Trader’s Joes is important to me because when I was a freshmen coming to Parsons my sister who graduated from NYU told me to go to Trader’s Joe whenever I needed groceries. It was one of the first places I visited when I started school. I remember buying my first bag of groceries and coming home excited to see what groceries in New York were like. I was so used to Hong Kong franchise markets I was extremely excited about the different foods offered in American markets. This year, I have been trying to budget and try to cook more at home since my dorm offers a kitchen. I think that Trader Joe’s is a special place for me because it offers so many different options for a reasonable price. It is not overly expensive but not too cheap that it seems unofficial. As a college student living in New York City, an average high quality meal (that are not fast foods) can cost anywhere between $9-20. I have also tried to live a healthier low budget lifestyle and therefore I try to search for cheaper alternatives for expensive foods. I think that Trader Joes offers the best produce in my budget range.
