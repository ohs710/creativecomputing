Font Facts Halyard
Designed by: Joshua Darden, Eben Sorkin and Lucas Sharp

“Halyard’s personality is at once familiar and pleasingly distinctive. Halyard’s familiarity results from cherry-picked elements of classic designed of the 19th and early 20th centuries by Schelter+Giesecke, Miller and Richard, and Morris Fuller Benton. Distinctiveness is achieved by bringing an almost impetuous vigor to Halyard’s shapes and through a willingness to adapt suprisingly historical forms into genuinely useful ones, which is so characterstic of Darden Studio designs. “

Halyard radically extends the typographic utility of now hyper ubiquitous category: the Grotesque Sans. Each of Halyard’s subfamilies were purpose built to perform optimally at its inteded optical size.

While starly different in appearance, when seen together, the Halyard superfamily maintains a consistent personality at their intended size creating the impressino of a single type design. The Halyard superfamily is beautifully robust and lively, ready to work at any size.”
