My journey to Trader Joe's is a 10 minute walk from 300 W 20th Street, which is also my dorm.
Trader Joe's is more than just a grocery store for me. It is a place where people gather to do one single action: cook. Whether it is for themselves or for someone else. Cooking is an act of love, self-care, respect and appreciation
